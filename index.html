Aleksandra Lelito

I am a Ph.D. student at the AGH University of Science and Technology in Krakow, Poland. I graduated at AGH at the Faculty of Applied Mathematics, with major in Mathematics in Technical and Natural Sciences. As a doctoral researcher I work in the domain of differential equations, with emphasis on applications of geometric methods (local symmetries or conservation laws, and their nonlocal analogoues examined in the framework of differential coverings) to integrable partial differential equations.
